Tater Tot Breakfast Casserole

Description:

This Tater Tot Breakfast Casserole is a delicious and hearty dish that combines crispy tater tots, savory sausage, fluffy eggs, and melted cheese. It's the perfect breakfast or brunch option for feeding a crowd or enjoying a comforting meal any day of the week.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 (32-ounce) bag frozen tater tots

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the breakfast sausage and chopped onion over medium heat until the sausage is browned and cooked through. Drain any excess fat.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, oregano, thyme, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  4. Spread the cooked sausage and onion mixture evenly in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese over the sausage mixture.
  5. Arrange the frozen tater tots in a single layer on top of the cheese.
  6. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the tater tots, making sure to cover all the tots.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese on top of the casserole.
  8. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
